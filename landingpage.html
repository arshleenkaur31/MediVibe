About MediVibe

MediVibe is an AI-powered voice assistant designed to make healthcare more human. Our mission is to simplify access to care by enabling patients to speak naturally to book appointments, get medication reminders, or receive instant symptom triage guidance. No waiting. No complicated forms. Just say it, and MediVibe understands.
